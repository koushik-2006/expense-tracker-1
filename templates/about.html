adjective

  1. discreetly cautious
    1. hesitant and vigilant about dangers and risks
    2. slow to grant, accept, or expend

noun /ˈɛk-spɛn-zoʊ/ 1. A smart digital companion for tracking, analyzing, and mastering personal expenses. 2. A tool designed to build better budgeting habits and help users make informed financial decisions. 3. The habit of spending mindfully, saving consistently, and living financially aware.

Expenzo is a clean, user-friendly expense tracker app that helps you manage your daily spending, visualize your financial habits, and stay on top of your budget goals. With real-time analytics, customizable budgets, and responsive design, Expenzo makes money management easy for everyone — students, professionals, and families alike

Development Details

Expenzo was developed by Koushik Sethuraman as a personal project with a passion for simplifying personal finance management. Development began in 2024, with the goal of creating a clean, intuitive, and user-friendly expense tracking app for students, professionals, and everyday users

Expenzo is built using HTML, CSS, and Vanilla JavaScript for the frontend, offering a responsive and lightweight experience across all devices. The backend (if implemented in future versions) is planned to be powered by Flask or Node.js, with integration options for cloud-based databases like Firebase.

Most of the visual assets, including the logo, icons, and UI elements, were custom-designed to match Expenzo’s clean and modern feel. Illustrations used across the landing pages are sourced from undraw.co, ensuring a cohesive and visually appealing interface.
